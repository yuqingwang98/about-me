About Me

Hey, I'm Yuqing, a London-based EdTech freelance teacher who started her journey of web development learning in 2022. I have a background in sustainable urban development, and code in Python and JavaScript.

I am passionate about learning new things and hope to apply my skills to create beautiful and functional Web apps.

Why Web Development?

My journey in tech started in learning data science because of my love of Python. Soon, I realised that I wanted to do something more than cleaning data and training models... I want to learn how to build something more practical, and fun.

For this reason, I started learning responsive web design on Free Codecamp and also completed The Odin Project's introduction track. I am now on track to finish University of Helsinki's Full Stack Open course by March, bringing me one step closer to achieve my goal to become a web developer.

However, it is not just about learning new skills. I also want to be able to connect with like-minded people and collaborate on projects. After a long period of hesitation, I have finally managed to complete the projects with the support of Founders and Coders' Wednesday meetup group.

I sincerely hope to continue to learn and grow with the community.
